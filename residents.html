Citizenship

Residents of Negros Oriental are mostly Filipinos. Others include Chinese, British, Amercian, Algerian, German and others.

Citizenship Number
1 Filipino 921,963
2 Chinese 378
3 British 188
4 Amercian 123
5 Algerian 17
6 German 13
7 Japanese 13
8 Indian 13
9 Australian 12
10 Others 72
11 Not Stated 464

Source: 1990 Census on Population

The table above provides the 1990 figures of the people residing in the province with their individiual citizenship. A big number of Chinese nationals residing in the province are naturalized Filipino citizens. The same table shows that the second largest number of citizens in the province is Chinese, followed by British then by the Americans. Australians are the least number of nationals living in Negros Oriental.
